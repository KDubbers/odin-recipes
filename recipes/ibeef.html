Italian Beef

Description

Slow cooker Italian Beef is the great equalizer, the old reliable, the thing you can confidently bring to any get together and have everyone asking you for the recipe by the end of the night. In short, itâ€™s exactly the kind of recipe we all need more of in our lives.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Giardiniera (blend of pickled veggies)
  • Pepperoncini
  • Italian Seasonings
  • Provolone Cheese
  • Hoagie Buns

Steps

  1. Let the meat rest at room temperature. Salt the beef.
  2. Sear the beef, then transfer to slow cooker.
  3. Deglaze the pan with beef broth, then add the liquid to the slow cooker.
  4. Stir the seasoning together, then sprinkle them over the beef.
  5. Add the peppers and giardiniera
  6. Cook on low for 8 to 10 hours. Shred the beef. Cook another 30 minutes.
  7. Toast the buns at 350 degrees for 5 to 7 minutes. Top the buns with beef, cheese, and additional pepperoncini and giardiniera. Serve.
