Hamburger

Description

These burgers offer a rich and meaty taste, with a punch of zesty herbs that can be customized with your preferred flavors. A few simple key ingredients help to consistently create juicy hamburger patties, no matter which cooking method you prefer.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground chuck
  • 1/2 cup crushed saltine crackers
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon black powder

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, add ground beef, crackers, egg, sauce, milk, salt, garlick powder, onion powder, and pepper. Mix by hand until the meat is smooth, but stop once the mixture looks even.
  2. Press the meat down in the bowl, into an even disk. use a knife to cut and divide the patty mixture into 6 1/3 pound patties, or 12 thinner patties.
  3. Line a baking sheet with foil to hold the patties. One at a time, gather form the mix into patties. Shape them just slightly larger than the buns you plan to use. Set the patties on the baking sheet. Use a spoon to press a dent in the center of each patty.
  4. Preheat grill to medium heat (350-400 degrees F.)
  5. Grill the patties for 3-4 minutes per side (2minutes for thin patties).
  6. Stack the hot patties on hamburger buns, and top with your favorite toppings.
  7. Serve Warm
